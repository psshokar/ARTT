ARTT Project

Accessible Research and Teaching Tools

Welcome to the ARTT Project - a website that provides resources for the visually impaired community.

Users help other users by giving their review on the accessibility of different tools. We strive to make this website a place where users can share their experiences, learn from others, and raise awareness on the accessibility of research and teaching tools. With the right resources and support, we hope that every user will be empowered to use technology in their everyday lives.
